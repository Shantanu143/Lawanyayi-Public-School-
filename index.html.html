About Us

Welcome to Lawanyayi Public School, where we believe in nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future. Our school is dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Vision

At Lawanyayi Public School, our mission is to offer a well-rounded education that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where each student can reach their full potential and become lifelong learners.


Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that sets the standard for excellence in teaching and learning. We strive to inspire students to become confident, responsible, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the future.

Meet Our Team

Our team of highly qualified teachers and staff are committed to providing a supportive and enriching learning environment. Each member of our team brings unique skills and experiences, ensuring that every student receives personalized attention and guidance.
